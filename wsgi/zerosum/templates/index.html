Track owes easily

Just enter the amount and the other persons e-mail address to save the owe. If you like, you can also add a subject so that you know why you owe someone when looking at it later.

Let them know

Whenever you add a new owe, the other person is informed about this via e-mail. They know that you will remember and can look it up any time.

Get a balance

Balances between two people are calculated based on all their owes. This makes exchanging money unnecessary in many cases when you pay for each other frequently.
